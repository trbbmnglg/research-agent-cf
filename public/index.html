About this app

Personalized AI News Agent — a self-correcting research assistant built to explore agentic AI patterns.

How it works — the agentic loop
plan → search → score → synthesize → reflect. After drafting a briefing, the reflect node critiques its own coverage, lists what's missing, and loops back to run targeted searches — repeating until coverage is solid or it hits your max research passes cap.
